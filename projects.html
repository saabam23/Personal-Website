Plug With GenZ

(November 2022 - Present)

Plug is a student led organization that I founded in 2021 for my Girl Scout Gold Award Project. The website acts as an activist toolkit for users and spreads awareness on mental health. The purpose of this project is to address the stigma, educate teens on life skills to improve their well-being, build various communities of people who share the same interests, and provide reliable education on topics that affect teens' mental health. This is one of my favorite website projects so far and I am excited to continue it. (Viewed better on a PC or laptop) Programming Languages: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery
